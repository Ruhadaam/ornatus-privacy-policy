# Privacy Policy for Ornatus
**Last updated: January 01, 2026**
This Privacy Policy describes how Ornatus ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "Service").
By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
## 1. Information We Collect
### 1.1 Personal Data
While using our Service, we may ask you to provide certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you, including but not limited to:
*   Email address (for account creation via Supabase Auth)
*   Usage Data
### 1.2 Usage Data
Usage Data is collected automatically when using the Service. This may include information such as your device's Internet Protocol address (IP address), browser type, operating system, distinct device ID, manufacturing data, and carrier usage.
### 1.3 Media and Camera
Our app specifically requires access to your device's **Camera** and **Photo Library** to function. This data is used solely for:
*   Uploading images of your clothing items to your private digital wardrobe.
*   Analyzing these images using Google Cloud Vision API to automatically categorize and tag your items (e.g., detecting color, type of clothing).
**We do not share your private photos publicly.** Your wardrobe images are stored securely on our servers (powered by Supabase) and are accessible only by you.
## 2. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services which may collect information used to identify you:
*   **Supabase:** Used for user authentication and database storage. [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)
*   **Google Cloud Vision API:** Used for image analysis and auto-tagging. Images are processed to extract features (labels, colors) and are not used by Google for training their models outside of this request context. [Google Cloud Privacy](https://cloud.google.com/terms/cloud-privacy-notice)
*   **AdMob (Google):** We may display advertisements provided by AdMob. AdMob uses identifiers (like Advertising ID) to serve personalized ads. [Google Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)
*   **RevenueCat:** Used to manage In-App Purchases and subscriptions. They collect purchase history information to validate your subscription status. [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)
*   **Expo:** As this app is built with Expo, it may collect some error logs or usage statistics to help us improve app stability.
